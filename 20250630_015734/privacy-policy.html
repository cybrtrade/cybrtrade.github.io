Privacy Policy for OnBrand LinkedIn API App

At OnBrand, we value the privacy of our users and their data. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect and how we use it in our LinkedIn API app.


Information We Collect

As a LinkedIn API app, we may collect the following information from our users:

- Basic profile information, such as name and headline

- Connections and network information, such as the number and names of connections

- Company information

Any other data that the user has authorised us to access through the LinkedIn API

We do not collect any sensitive or personal information from our users without their explicit consent.


How We Use Information

We use the information collected from the LinkedIn API to provide our users with personalized and relevant content, such as job listings and company information. We may also use this information to analyze user behavior and improve our app's functionality and performance.

We do not share or sell any user data to third parties without their explicit consent, except as required by law or in response to a valid legal request.


Security

We take the security of our users' data seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of data.

User Control

Users have full control over the data that they share with our app through the LinkedIn API. They may revoke our access to their data at any time through the LinkedIn platform or by contacting us directly.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our app or to comply with legal or regulatory requirements. Users will be notified of any material changes to this policy and their continued use of our app constitutes their acceptance of the updated policy.

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices or this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@onbrand.com.
